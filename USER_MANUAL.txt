1. CREATING AN ACCOUNT

Follow these simple steps to create your Ebakunado account:

STEP 1: PERSONAL INFORMATION

1. Open the app and tap "Create Account?" on the login screen.

2. Fill in your personal details:

   - First Name: Enter your first name
   - Last Name: Enter your last name
   - Email Address: Enter a valid email address (e.g., yourname@email.com)
   - Phone Number: Enter your phone number (e.g., 09123456789)
   - Gender: Select your gender from the dropdown (Male, Female, or Other)

3. Verify your phone number:

   - Tap the "Send OTP" button
   - You will receive a 6-digit code via SMS on your phone
   - A popup will appear asking for the code
   - Enter the 6-digit code you received
   - The code expires in 5 minutes
   - Tap "Verify" to confirm
   - If you don't receive the code, wait for the timer to expire and tap "Resend OTP"

4. Once verified, tap "Next" to continue

STEP 2: ADDRESS INFORMATION

1. Enter your complete address:

   - Province: Enter your province name
   - City / Municipality: Enter your city or municipality
   - Barangay: Enter your barangay
   - Purok: Enter your purok

2. Tap "Next" to continue

STEP 3: SECURITY (PASSWORD)

1. Create a strong password:

   - Password: Enter a password that meets all requirements:

     - At least 8 characters long
     - Contains at least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
     - Contains at least one lowercase letter (a-z)
     - Contains at least one number (0-9)
     - Contains at least one special character (!@#$%^&*)
     - Not a common password (like "password" or "123456")

   - Confirm Password: Enter the same password again

2. Agree to Terms:

   - Check the box to agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
   - You can tap on the links to read them

3. Tap "Create Account" button

4. Success! You will see a success message and be redirected to the login screen


2. LOGGING IN

Once you have created your account, follow these steps to log in:

1. Open the app - You will see the login screen

2. Enter your credentials:

   - Email or Phone Number: Enter the email address or phone number you used when creating your account
   - Password: Enter your password
   - You can tap the eye icon to show/hide your password

3. Tap the "Login" button

4. If login is successful:

   - You will be taken to the home screen
   - You may see a message about battery optimization for notifications (Android only)

5. If login fails:
   - Check that your email/phone and password are correct
   - Make sure you have an internet connection
   - If you forgot your password, see the "Forgot Password" section below


3. FORGOT PASSWORD

If you forgot your password, don't worry! Follow these steps to reset it:

STEP 1: REQUEST VERIFICATION CODE

1. On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?"

2. Enter your email or phone number:

   - Enter the email address or phone number you used when creating your account
   - Example: yourname@email.com or 09123456789

3. Tap "Send Verification Code"

4. You will receive a 6-digit code:

   - If you entered an email: Check your email inbox
   - If you entered a phone number: Check your SMS messages

5. You will automatically be taken to the verification screen

STEP 2: VERIFY THE CODE

1. Enter the 6-digit code:

   - Type the code you received in the text field
   - The code expires in 5 minutes (you'll see a countdown timer)

2. Tap "Verify Code"

3. If the code is correct:

   - You'll see a success message
   - You'll be taken to the password reset screen

4. If you didn't receive the code:
   - Wait for the timer to reach 0:00
   - Tap "Resend" to request a new code

STEP 3: CREATE NEW PASSWORD

1. Enter your new password:

   - New Password: Create a password that meets all requirements:

     - At least 8 characters long
     - Contains at least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
     - Contains at least one lowercase letter (a-z)
     - Contains at least one number (0-9)
     - Contains at least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

   - You can tap the eye icon to show/hide your password
   - Watch the password requirements list - they will turn green as you meet each requirement

2. Confirm your new password:

   - Confirm Password: Enter the same password again
   - Make sure both passwords match

3. Tap "Reset Password"

4. Success!
   - You'll see a success message
   - Tap "Go to Login" to return to the login screen
   - Log in with your new password


4. DASHBOARD

The Dashboard is your main screen after logging in. It shows an overview of your children's immunization status.

OVERVIEW CARDS

At the top of the dashboard, you'll see four cards showing:

1. Total Children: The number of children registered in your account
2. Upcoming Today: Number of vaccinations scheduled for today
3. Upcoming: Total number of upcoming vaccinations
4. Missed: Total number of missed vaccinations

FILTERING CHILDREN

Below the overview cards, you can filter children by:

- Upcoming: Shows children with upcoming vaccination schedules
- Missed: Shows children who have missed vaccinations

Tap on a filter chip to view the filtered list.

CHILDREN LIST

The dashboard displays a list of your children with their immunization information:

For Upcoming Vaccinations:

- Child's name
- Vaccine name and dose number
- Scheduled date
- QR code icon (if available) - tap to view the QR code
- "View Schedule" button - tap to see the full vaccination schedule

For Missed Vaccinations:

- Child's name
- Number of missed vaccinations
- Closest missed vaccination details:
  - Vaccine name and dose number
  - Scheduled date
  - Catch-up date (if available)
- QR code icon (if available)
- "View Schedule" button

VIEWING QR CODE

1. Tap the QR code icon next to a child's name
2. A popup will show the QR code for that child
3. This QR code can be scanned by healthcare providers

VIEWING FULL SCHEDULE

1. Tap the "View Schedule" button on any child card
2. You'll see the complete vaccination schedule for that child
3. This includes all past, upcoming, and missed vaccinations


5. ADD CHILD

You can add a child to your account in two ways:

METHOD 1: USING FAMILY CODE (QUICK LINK)

If your BHW (Barangay Health Worker) or Midwife has given you a Family Code:

1. Open the menu (tap the hamburger icon in the top left)
2. Tap "Add Child"
3. In the "Have a Family Code?" section:
   - Enter the Family Code (e.g., FAM-ABC123)
   - Tap "Link Child"
4. If the code is valid, the child will be linked to your account immediately
5. You'll see a success message

METHOD 2: REGISTER NEW CHILD

If you don't have a Family Code, you can register a new child:

1. Open the menu (tap the hamburger icon in the top left)
2. Tap "Add Child"
3. Scroll down to the "Child Registration Form" section
4. Fill in all required information:

   Basic Child Information:

   - First Name * (required)
   - Last Name * (required)
   - Gender (Male/Female)
   - Birth Date * (required)
   - Place of Birth
   - Address (Province, City/Municipality, Barangay, Purok) * (required)

   Birth Information:

   - Birth Weight
   - Birth Height
   - Delivery Type (Normal/Cesarean)
   - Birth Order (Single/Twin/Triplet)
   - Birth Attendant (Doctor/Nurse/Midwife/Other)
   - Blood Type

   Parent Information:

   - Mother's Name
   - Father's Name

   Health Information:

   - Allergies
   - Last Menstrual Period (LMP) Date
   - Family Planning Method
   - Newborn Screening Date
   - Place of Newborn Screening

   Vaccination History:

   - Select any vaccines the child has already received
   - Check the boxes for vaccines already given

   Baby's Card:

   - Tap "Upload Baby's Card"
   - Choose to take a photo or select from gallery
   - This is required for registration

5. Tap "Submit Registration"
6. Success! Your child registration request will be sent for approval
7. You'll receive a confirmation message
8. Once approved by a BHW/Midwife, the child will appear in your "My Children" list

Note: Fields marked with * are required. Make sure to fill them in before submitting.


6. MY CHILDREN

View and manage all your registered children:

ACCESSING MY CHILDREN

1. Open the menu (tap the hamburger icon in the top left)
2. Tap "My Children"

FILTERING CHILDREN

At the top of the screen, you can filter by:

- Approved Children: Children that have been approved and are active
- Pending Registration: Children waiting for approval

Select a filter from the dropdown to view the filtered list.

VIEWING CHILD INFORMATION

Each child card shows:

- Child's name
- Status badge:
  - Green "Approved" badge for approved children
  - Orange "Pending" badge for pending registrations
- Registration date
- "View" button: Tap to see the child's full record

VIEWING CHILD RECORD

1. Tap the "View" button on any child card
2. You'll see the complete child record including:
   - Personal information
   - Birth details
   - Vaccination schedule
   - Vaccination history
   - Health records

PULL TO REFRESH

- Pull down on the screen to refresh the list
- This will update the status of pending registrations


7. APPROVED REQUESTS

View and download Baby Cards for approved child registrations:

ACCESSING APPROVED REQUESTS

1. Open the menu (tap the hamburger icon in the top left)
2. Tap "Approved Requests"

VIEWING APPROVED REQUESTS

The screen shows a list of all your approved child registration requests. Each entry displays:

- Child's name
- Approval date
- Status: "Approved"
- Download button: Tap to download the Baby Card PDF

DOWNLOADING BABY CARD

1. Find the child you want to download the Baby Card for
2. Tap the "Download" button (or download icon)
3. A loading dialog will appear
4. The PDF will be saved to your device's Downloads folder
5. You'll see a success message with the file location
6. You can open the PDF using any PDF viewer app

Note:

- Baby Cards are only available for approved requests
- If a request is still pending, the download button will be disabled
- The file will be named: BabyCard_[ChildName].pdf

PULL TO REFRESH

- Pull down on the screen to refresh the list
- This will check for newly approved requests


8. SETTINGS

Manage your profile, change password, and configure app settings:

ACCESSING SETTINGS

1. Open the menu (tap the hamburger icon in the top left)
2. Tap "Settings"

PROFILE INFORMATION

View and Edit Your Profile:

1. Your profile photo and basic info are shown at the top
2. To change your profile photo:

   - Tap the camera icon on your profile picture
   - Choose "Take Photo" or "Choose from Gallery"
   - The photo will upload automatically

3. To edit your information:
   - Update any of these fields:
     - First Name * (required)
     - Last Name * (required)
     - Email * (required)
     - Phone Number
     - Gender
     - Place/Address
     - PhilHealth Number
     - NHTS (National Household Targeting System)
   - Tap the Save icon in the top right corner
   - You'll see a success message when saved

Note: Fields marked with * are required.

CHANGE PASSWORD

1. In the Security section, toggle the switch to "Change Password"
2. Enter your Current Password
3. Enter your New Password (must meet requirements)
4. Confirm New Password (must match the new password)
5. Tap the Save icon in the top right corner
6. Your password will be updated

Password Requirements:

- At least 6 characters long
- Make sure both new password fields match

NOTIFICATIONS & PERMISSIONS

1. Tap on the "Notifications & Permissions" card
2. You'll see the status of:

   - Daily Check Time: When the app checks for notifications
   - Notifications: Whether notifications are allowed
   - Battery Optimization: Whether battery optimization is disabled

3. To configure notifications:
   - Tap the card to open notification settings
   - Set your preferred daily check time
   - Enable/disable notifications
   - Configure battery optimization settings

Note: For notifications to work properly, especially when the app is closed, you may need to disable battery optimization for the app (Android only).

APP INFORMATION

The App Information section shows:

- Version: Current app version (e.g., 1.0.0)

LOGOUT

1. Scroll to the bottom of the menu
2. Tap "Logout"
3. A confirmation dialog will appear
4. Tap "Logout" to confirm or "Cancel" to stay logged in
5. You'll be returned to the login screen


TIPS AND TROUBLESHOOTING

TIPS:

- Keep your password safe: Don't share your password with anyone
- Use a strong password: Follow all password requirements for better security
- Check your internet connection: Make sure you have a stable internet connection
- Check spam folder: If using email, check your spam/junk folder for verification codes
- Keep your app updated: Update to the latest version for new features and bug fixes
- Check notifications regularly: Review your dashboard daily for upcoming vaccinations
- Save your Family Code: If you receive a Family Code from your BHW/Midwife, keep it safe
- Upload clear photos: When uploading baby's card, make sure the photo is clear and readable
- Backup your Baby Cards: Download and save Baby Card PDFs for your records

COMMON ISSUES:

Problem: "OTP not received"

- Solution: Wait for the timer to expire and tap "Resend OTP"
- Make sure your phone number or email is correct
- Check your SMS messages or email inbox (including spam folder)

Problem: "Password doesn't meet requirements"

- Solution: Make sure your password has:
  - At least 8 characters
  - One uppercase letter
  - One lowercase letter
  - One number
  - One special character (!@#$%^&*)

Problem: "Incorrect email or password"

- Solution:
  - Double-check your email/phone and password
  - Make sure you're using the correct account
  - Try resetting your password if you're unsure

Problem: "Network error"

- Solution:
  - Check your internet connection
  - Try again after a few moments
  - Make sure you have mobile data or Wi-Fi enabled

Problem: "Family Code not working"

- Solution:
  - Make sure you entered the code correctly (case-sensitive)
  - Contact your BHW/Midwife to verify the code
  - Try registering the child manually instead

Problem: "Child registration pending for a long time"

- Solution:
  - Registration requests need approval from a BHW/Midwife
  - Contact your local health center to check the status
  - Make sure all required information was filled correctly

Problem: "Cannot download Baby Card"

- Solution:
  - Make sure the request has been approved
  - Check your device storage space
  - Grant storage permissions if prompted
  - Try again after a few moments

Problem: "Notifications not working"

- Solution:
  - Check notification settings in your device settings
  - Make sure notifications are enabled for the app
  - Disable battery optimization for the app (Android)
  - Set a daily check time in Settings > Notifications & Permissions

Problem: "Cannot upload profile photo"

- Solution:
  - Grant camera/gallery permissions when prompted
  - Make sure the photo is not too large
  - Try taking a new photo or selecting a different image
  - Check your internet connection


NEED HELP?

If you continue to experience issues:

- Make sure you have the latest version of the app
- Check that your device has a stable internet connection
- Try closing and reopening the app
- Restart your device if problems persist
